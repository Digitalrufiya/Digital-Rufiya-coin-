Arabic Text

بِسْمِ اللّٰهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
إِنَّا أَنزَلْنَاهُ فِي لَيْلَةِ الْقَدْرِ
وَمَا أَدْرَاكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ الْقَدْرِ
لَيْلَةُ الْقَدْرِ خَيْرٌ مِّنْ أَلْفِ شَهْرٍ
تَنَزَّلُ الْمَلَائِكَةُ وَالرُّوحُ فِيهَا بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِم مِّن كُلِّ أَمْرٍ
سَلَامٌ هِيَ حَتَّىٰ مَطْلَعِ الْفَجْرِ

Translation

1. Indeed, We sent the Qur’an down during the Night of Decree.
2. And what can make you know what is the Night of Decree?
3. The Night of Decree is better than a thousand months.
4. The angels and the Spirit descend therein by permission of their Lord for every matter.
5. Peace it is until the emergence of dawn.

Meaning and Importance

  • Qadr means Divine Decree — all that will happen is written by Allah’s knowledge and wisdom.
  • Laylatul Qadr is a night of immense power, peace, and destiny — better than 83 years of worship.
  • The Qur’an was revealed in full to the lowest heaven on this night — then gradually to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
  • This night is when all matters of the year are decreed and assigned by the command of Allah.

Mathematical Miracles

  • Surah Al-Qadr has 5 verses, 30 words — matching 30 days of Ramadan.
  • It contains exactly 114 letters — the same number of Surahs in the Qur’an.
  • "A thousand months" = 83 years and 4 months — a divine mathematical lesson.

Reflections

The message of Al-Qadr is clear: your life, your breath, your future — all are written. Submit to the One who writes. Worship Him in sincerity, and He will elevate you beyond a thousand months. In the hour of darkness, this Night of Power gives light.
