Whitepaper: Digital Rufiya Coin (DRF) Revolution Ecosystem

Introduction

The DRF Revolution Ecosystem is a decentralized humanitarian platform designed to empower believers with tools, finance, and knowledge. Founded by Hassan Fayaz, this initiative aims to provide a fully decentralized economic and educational system aligned with Islamic values.

Core Tokens

  • Digital Rufiya Coin (DRF): Main ecosystem token used for transactions, staking, wallet features, and utilities.
  • DRFM Token: A dedicated charity token used for DRF charity-based platforms, zakat/sadaqah distributions, and rewards.
  • Bitcoin Pound Token (symbol: £): Tokenized value pegged to GBP, used for liquidity and donations.

Ecosystem Platforms

  • DRF Wallet: BSC-compatible wallet supporting DRF, DRFM, USDC, USDT with send, receive, QR codes, BscScan integration.
  • DRF Swap: Token swap interface with real-time gas estimation, liquidity lock support.
  • Charity System: Direct charity requests, charity game rewards, and donation tracking.
  • DRFMedia: Social media platform for believers to post videos, text, and images.
  • DRFTube: Decentralized video-sharing platform with IPFS support and upload permission control.
  • DRFMPlayer: Halal audio/music upload and streaming platform for artists to share nasheeds or halal content.
  • DRF University: Knowledge base for believers including videos, texts, Quranic references, and courses.
  • DRF Knowledge Base: Islamic encyclopedia powered by AI and Quranic AI system.
  • Quranic Intelligence System: AI system interpreting questions based on Quran and authentic Hadiths.
  • Final Hour Tracker: A platform reminding of Akhira, end-time signs, and preparation knowledge.
  • RedotPay ATM Integration: Enables crypto to fiat ATM withdrawals globally.
  • DRF Explorer: Blockchain explorer customized for DRF-related contracts and token activity.
  • Legitimate Mining Platforms: Trusted referrals where users can earn from mining and DRF gains liquidity through referral rewards.
  • DRF Browser: Built-in browser to access all ecosystem DApps and tools in one secured place.

Charity Distribution Logic

  1. Users request help via the DRF platform or directly.
  2. DRF team reviews and approves requests.
  3. Liquidity is added to DRFM and £ tokens for withdrawal or DRF swap exchange.

Founder

Hassan Fayaz — Founder, visionary, and full-stack developer of the DRF Revolution Ecosystem. All platforms were built with mobile phone only and funded via patience and tawakkul in Allah.

Security & Privacy

  • All video content on DRFTube uses IPFS with JWT-based access control.
  • Admin access limited to digitalrufiyacoin@gmail.com for deletion or sensitive changes.
  • Frontend protected with custom auth.js Firebase login logic.

Tokenomics (Summary)

  • DRF: Total Supply 100 million. Used across the entire ecosystem.
  • DRFM: 10 million supply reserved for charity and community use.
  • £ Token: Minted on-demand and liquidity-backed for charity only.
